Software Engineer – Tester

We are looking for interested & talented Software Engineer – Dot Net to join our team. Candidates should enjoy working in a challenging environment; have the enthusiasm and ambition to develop & complete projects to the highest standards. Candidates with 1-3 years of experience in Asp.Net will be considered.

Job Description

  • Dynamic Engineers / Graduates & Post Graduates with 1-3 years of experience in the IT development section
  • Exposure to Artificial intelligence for automation, QA automation, Agile and DevOps, Security matters, Performance engineering, IoT and big data testing, Blockchain testing.
  • Knowledge on web services, Specific on Restful, Messaging experience (Rabbit MQ), Micro services and API based development
  • Good technical knowledge & experience
  • Experience of the full software development lifecycle – from business / systems analysis, thorough requirements gathering and functional specification authoring to development.

Responsibilities

  • A clear understanding of customer-centered design and testing.
  • Test and ensure the requirement with adherence to international coding standards
  • Practice self QA to develop quality application.
  • Implement best practices, standards and procedures including quality and delivery methodologies.
  • Test the Coding’s with the client for requirement analysis
  • Frequent status updates through client and internal communication
  • Handle the team of testers / mentoring the juniors by task briefing, review the tasks & giving feedback
